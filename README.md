# Customer-Insights-Dashboard

### 1. Short Description / Purpose
Developed an interactive Customer Insights Dashboard using the AdventureWorksDW dataset to analyze customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and revenue contribution. The dashboard provides meaningful insights into customer segmentation, family-based purchasing patterns, age-group revenue trends, and top-performing customers to support data-driven business decisions.

### 2. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:<br>

📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used to build the dashboard and reports.<br>
📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.<br>
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to create calculated columns, measures, KPIs, and dynamic filtering logic.<br>
📝 Data Modeling – Relationships were created between fact and dimension tables for proper aggregation and cross-filtering.<br>
📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .pdf/.png for dashboard preview and reporting.

### 3. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to understand customer purchasing behavior, revenue distribution, and demographic patterns from raw transactional data. Without a proper analytical system, identifying valuable customers and revenue-driving segments becomes difficult.

Key questions such as:<br>

What is the average age of customers?<br>
What is the total customer base?<br>
Which customers are VIP, Loyal, or Periodic Buyers?<br>
Do customers with children contribute more revenue than those without children?<br>
Who are the top-performing customers?<br>
How does revenue vary across gender and age groups?<br>

…are difficult to answer efficiently using raw data alone.

### • Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive analytical dashboard that enables:<br>

Customer behavior analysis<br>
Revenue trend identification<br>
Customer segmentation<br>
Demographic-based insights<br>
Dynamic filtering for deeper analysis<br>

The main objective was to transform raw customer and sales data into actionable business intelligence.

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

- Key KPIs<br>
The dashboard displays major KPIs including:<br>

  Total Customers (18K)<br>
  Total Revenue (29.36M)<br>
  Average Customer Age (46.45)<br>

  These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.<br>

- Esteemed Buyers (Horizontal Bar Chart)<br>
Shows the top revenue-generating customers based on ranking analysis.
This helps identify high-value customers contributing maximum revenue.<br>

- Family-Based Revenue Analysis (Donut Chart)<br>
Compares revenue contribution between:<br>

   Customers With Children (70.59%)<br>
   Customers Without Children (29.41%)<br>

   This reveals how family status impacts customer purchasing behavior.<br>

- Revenue Segmented by Age-Bracket (Column Chart)<br>
Displays revenue contribution across different age groups:<br>

   21–30<br>
   31–40<br>
   41–50<br>
   51–60<br>
   61+<br>

   This helps identify which age group contributes the most revenue.<br>

- Revenue Contribution by Gender (Cards + Visuals)<br>
Compares revenue generated by:<br>

    Male Customers (14.55M)<br>
    Female Customers (14.8M)<br>

   This helps analyze gender-based purchasing patterns.<br>

- Customer Profiling (Bar Chart)<br>
Customers are categorized into:<br>

   VIP Customers<br>
  Loyal Customers<br>
  Periodic Buyers<br>

  Segmentation is based on order frequency and purchase value, helping identify customer loyalty levels.<br>

- Interactive Filter Panel<br>
The dashboard includes slicers for:<br>

   Country<br>
   Gender<br>
   Year<br>
   Customer Category<br>

   These filters allow users to perform dynamic and focused analysis.<br>

### • Business Impact & Insights
- Improved Customer Segmentation: The dashboard clearly identified VIP, Loyal, and Periodic Buyers, helping businesses focus on high-value customer retention strategies.
- Better Demographic Understanding: Analysis showed that customers with children contributed significantly higher revenue than customers without children.
- Targeted Marketing Opportunities: Age-group and gender analysis revealed customer segments contributing maximum revenue, enabling more personalized marketing strategies.
- Enhanced Decision-Making: Interactive slicers and dynamic visuals allow faster business analysis and support data-driven strategic planning.
